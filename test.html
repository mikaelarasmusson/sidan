The Slim Shady LP

Released February 1999

Producers: Eminem, Lyrics Dr.Dre, Music & Lyrics The Bass Brothers, Producing & Music

The Slim Shady LP is the second studio album and major label debut by American rapper Eminem. It was released on February 23, 1999, by Aftermath Entertainment and Interscope Records. Recorded in Ferndale, Michigan following Eminem's recruitment by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine, the album features production from Dr. Dre, the Bass Brothers, and Eminem himself.

The Slim Shady LP debuted at number two on the Billboard 200, just below TLC's FanMail, and number one on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart.

The album was a critical and commercial success, with critics praising Eminem for his unique lyrical style, dark humor lyrics, and eccentric personality.

The Marshall Mathers LP

Released May 2000

Producers: Eminem, Lyrics Dr.Dre, Music & Lyrics The 45 King, Producing & Music

The Marshall Mathers LP is the third studio album by American rapper Eminem, released on May 23, 2000, by Aftermath Entertainment and Interscope Records. The album was produced mostly by Dr. Dre and Eminem, along with the 45 King, the Bass Brothers, and Mel-Man. Recorded over a two-month period in several studios around Detroit, the album features more introspective lyricism, including Eminem's thoughts on his rise from rags to riches, the criticism of his music, and his estrangement from his family and wife.

Side One

  1. Public Service Announcement 2000
  2. Kill You
  3. Stan (with Dido)
  4. Paul (skit)
  5. Who Knew
  6. Steve Berman (skit)

Side Two

  1. The Way I Am
  2. The Real Slim Shady
  3. Remember Me?
  4. I'm Back
  5. Marshall Mathers
  6. Ken Kaniff (skit)

The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200, staying atop for eight consecutive weeks. A significant commercial success compared to the release of The Slim Shady LP just the previous year, the album sold 1.78 million copies in its first week, which made it among the fastest-selling studio albums in the United States.

The Marshall Mathers LP has been included in numerous all-time lists and is widely regarded as Eminem's greatest album.

Eminem and Dr.Dre in the studio.

The Marshall Mathers LP was nominated for Album of the Year and won Best Rap Album at the 2001 Grammy Awards. Rolling Stone named it the best album of 2000.
